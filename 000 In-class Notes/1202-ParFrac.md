# partial fraction
$$
\begin{aligned}
\int \frac{5x - 3}{x^{2} - 2x -3}\ dx & = \int \frac{5x-3}{(x-3)(x-1)}\ dx \\
 & = \int \left( \frac{2}{x + 1} + \frac{3 }{x - 3} \right)\ dx \\
 & = 2 \ln \left| x + 1 \right|  + 3 \ln \left| x - 3 \right| + C
\end{aligned}
$$
