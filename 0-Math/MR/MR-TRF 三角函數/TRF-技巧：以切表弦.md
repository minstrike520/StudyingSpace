# 以切表弦
$$
\tan 2\theta = \frac{2\tan \theta}{1-\tan ^{2} \theta}
$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sin 2\theta = \frac{2\tan\theta}{1+\tan \theta} &  & \cos 2\theta = \frac{1-\tan \theta}{1+\tan \theta}
\end{aligned}
$$
