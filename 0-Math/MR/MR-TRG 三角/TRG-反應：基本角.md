(狹義角 $0\degree < \theta < 90\degree$適用)
餘弦邊界
$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Max:}\ \cos 0\degree = 1 &  & \text{min:}\ \cos 90\degree = 0
\end{aligned}
$$
正弦邊界
$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Max:}\ \sin 90\degree = 1 &  & \text{min:}\ \sin 0\degree = 0
\end{aligned}
$$
