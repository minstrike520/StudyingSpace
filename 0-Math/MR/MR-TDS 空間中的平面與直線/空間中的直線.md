# 空間中的直線
## 參數式
$$
\left\{\begin{aligned}
 & x = x_{0}+at \\
 & y = y_{0}+bt \\
 & z = z_{0}+ct
\end{aligned}\right. ,\ \ t \in \mathbb{R}
$$

## 比例式
$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{a} = \frac{y-y_{0}}{b} = \frac{z-z_{0}}{c}
$$

## 兩面式
$$
\left\{\begin{aligned}
 & E_{1}: a_{1}x+b_{1}y+c_{1}z+d_{1} = 0 \\
 & E_{2}: a_{2}x+b_{2}y+c_{2}z+d_{2} = 0
\end{aligned}\right.
$$
