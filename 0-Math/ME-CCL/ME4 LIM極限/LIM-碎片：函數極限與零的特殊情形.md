函數連續且在該點**有不定因式** -> 代入值為零
$$
\text{continuous}(f, a) \land \lim_{ x \to a } \frac{f(x)}{x-a}\ \text{exists} \implies f(a) = 0
$$
$$
\lnot\ [ \text{continuous}(f, a)] \land \lim_{ x \to a } \frac{f(x)}{x-a}\ \text{exists} \not\implies f(a) = 0
$$
