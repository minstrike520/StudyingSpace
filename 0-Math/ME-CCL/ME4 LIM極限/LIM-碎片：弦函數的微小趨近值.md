$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{ \theta \to 0 } \frac{\sin\theta}{\theta} = 1 &  & \lim_{ \theta \to 0 } \frac{\tan\theta}{\theta} = 1 &  & \lim_{ \theta \to 0 } \frac{\tan\theta}{\sin\theta} = 1
\end{aligned}
$$
實務應用
$$
\theta \to 0 \implies \sin\theta \approx \tan\theta \approx \theta
$$
