v = f\lambda & & f = \frac{v}{\lambda} &  & \lambda = \frac{v}{f}
\end{aligned}
$$

<p align="center">管樂器共鳴</p>
?
$$
\begin{aligned}
L_{m} = \frac{m}{4}\lambda + L_{0} &  & L_{n} = \frac{n}{2}\lambda + L_{0}
\end{aligned}
$$
註釋：左為閉管，右為開管；$L_{0}$ 為管口修正量；$m \in 2\mathbb{N}-1,\ n \in \mathbb{N}$ 代表諧音數
