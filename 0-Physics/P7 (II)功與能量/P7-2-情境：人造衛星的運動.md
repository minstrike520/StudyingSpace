> [!Note] 反應：「圓軌道運行+能量」 #做題反應
> $$E = \frac{1}{2}U = -K$$
^note1

- 推導 
$$
\begin{aligned}
 & F_{g} = \frac{GMm}{r^{2}},\ F_{c} = \frac{mv^{2}}{r},\ F_{g} = F_{c} \\
 & \to\ v^{2} = \frac{GM}{r} \\
 & \to\ K = \frac{1}{2} mv^{2} = \frac{GMm}{2r} \\
 & \to\ U = - \frac{GMm}{r} \\ \\
 & \to\ E = K + U = - \frac{GMm}{2r}\\
 & \therefore E = \frac{1}{2}U = -K\ \blacksquare
\end{aligned}
$$
