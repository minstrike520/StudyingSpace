# 解答
(依照原題上標記)
$$
\begin{aligned}
 & h_{1} = 5, (h_{1}+h_{2}) = 45, h_{2} = 40 \\
 & 16({h_{1}}^{2}+d^{2}) = {h_{2}}^{2}+(2d)^{2} \\
 & 400 + 16d^{2} = 1600 + 4d^{2} \\
 & 12d^{2} = 1200, d = 10\ \blacksquare
\end{aligned}
$$
